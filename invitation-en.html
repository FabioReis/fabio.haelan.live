Wedding Pray

Almighty God! Thank you for allowing us to have a Fabio and Haelan ceremony today.

Lord who presides over every man! May you always bless the future of this beautiful couple that are here today to celebrate their love, which is just the beginning of the long path.

First, for their lives:
Let them always think about what it means to love each other, so today is always happier than yesterday and tomorrow is happier than today. Let them live every day with words and actions of love, so that they can bloom the flower of happiness.

Second, for their work:
Let Fabio and Haelan grow into professionals who challenge themselves through constant self-improvement, becoming creative and cooperative professionals, contributing to the development of their schools and companies.

Third, for their social life:
As members of the community, let them live a happy life within their community, caring and being grateful for everyone around them, so that we can all achieve a healthy and happy society.

In particular, please keep and support Fabio Reis, who is now in South Korea, far away from his home country, Brazil, to challenge and pioneer his own life.

God of mercy! We pray for Fabio and Haelan to be blessed with all the things, even the ones they have do not expect for.

In addition, I would like to ask blessings to all those who, despite the Corona virus, have used their precious time to join this wedding today.

I pray for all this through our Lord Jesus Christ. Amen.

10 October 2020

Fausto Reis • Luzia Reis
Park Ki-jin Antonio • Jeong Jeom-ok Antonia
